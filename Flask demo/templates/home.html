Welcome to RVCE Library

The RVCE Library is dedicated to fostering a vibrant learning environment by providing access to a vast collection of books, journals, e-resources, and more. With state-of-the-art facilities and digital services, we aim to support academic excellence and research initiatives.

About Us

Established in 1963, the RVCE Library serves as the intellectual hub of the campus. Our mission is to provide resources and services that inspire learning and research, empowering the RVCE community to achieve its goals.

Key Features:

  • A collection of over 50,000 books and journals
  • Access to online databases and e-books
  • Digital library services
  • Comfortable reading spaces

Our Services

  • Book Lending
  • Research Assistance
  • Inter-Library Loan
  • Access to E-Journals and Databases
  • Study Room Reservations
